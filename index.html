WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Nohaya Business Solutions, Ethiopia's premier import-export International marketing and consulting firm.

At Nohaya Business Solutions, we specialize in helping businesses around the world to expand their reach and increase their profits through international trade. By leveraging our extensive network of international contacts, comprehensive knowledge of the international market, and cutting-edge marketing strategies, we’re able to provide our clients with the resources they need to succeed in the global marketplace.

Whether you are looking to expand your business into the global market or just starting out, our consultants can help you navigate the international trade landscape. From market research and compliance to product sourcing and product selection, we have the expertise to ensure your success.

We also provide specialized services such as customs clearance, logistics, and consulting on international trade regulations. Our team is always up-to-date with the latest trends in Ethiopia and global trade, so you can trust us for the best advice on how to maximize profits and minimize risks.

At Nohaya, our goal is to provide our clients with the best possible advice and guidance for their import-export business. We understand that each business has unique needs, and we strive to tailor our services to meet those needs.

​ We understand the complexities of international trade. Our team of experienced professionals have years of experience in the import-export industry, marketing and are ready to provide you with the expert advice and guidance you need to succeed.

OUR TEAM


Our team at Nohaya Business Solutions is a diverse and talented group of professionals who are committed to driving the growth and success of our clients' businesses. With their expertise in various areas of international trade, they work together to provide innovative solutions, excellent customer service, and seamless trade operations. We take pride in our team's dedication, knowledge, and passion for international trade, and we are confident in their ability to deliver exceptional results for our clients. ​

Senior Leadership Team

Our senior leadership team consists of industry experts with years of experience in international trade. They bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table to guide our company and clients towards success. Our leaders are committed in providing strategic direction, ensuring excellent customer service, and fostering strong relationships with partners and stakeholders.

Sales and Business Development

Our sales and business development team is responsible for identifying and capitalizing on business opportunities in the international trade market. They are equipped with extensive market research skills, negotiation skills, and a deep understanding of international trade regulations. This team works closely with clients to understand their needs, develop innovative solutions, and establish profitable trade networks.

Logistics and Supply Chain Management

Our logistics and supply chain management team is responsible for ensuring the smooth and efficient flow of goods and services across borders. They handle everything from transportation and warehousing to inventory management and distribution. This team works tirelessly to optimize supply chain efficiencies, reduce costs, mitigate risks, and enhance customer satisfaction.

Finance and Accounting

Our finance and accounting team ensures that all financial aspects of our international trade operations are managed efficiently and accurately. They handle financial planning, budgeting, invoicing, and financial reporting. With their expertise, they help our clients make informed financial decisions and maintain a strong financial position.

Legal and Compliance

Our legal and compliance team ensures that our international trade operations are conducted in full compliance with national and international laws and regulations. They stay updated with the ever-changing legal landscape and provide legal advice and guidance to our clients. This team works closely with our clients to navigate complex legal requirements, mitigate risks, and resolve any legal issues that arise..

Customer Support

Our customer support team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and support to our clients. They are the first point of contact for any inquiries, concerns, or issues that our clients may have. Our customer support team is committed to ensuring client satisfaction and building strong, long-term relationships.

Operations and Administration

Our operations and administration team plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth day-to-day running of our international trade operations. They handle administrative tasks, project management, and process improvement. This team ensures that all internal processes are streamlined and aligned with our clients' needs to achieve.
