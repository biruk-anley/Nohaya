WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Nohaya Business Solutions, Ethiopia's premier import-export International marketing and consulting firm.

At Nohaya Business Solutions, we specialize in helping businesses around the world to expand their reach and increase their profits through international trade. By leveraging our extensive network of international contacts, comprehensive knowledge of the international market, and cutting-edge marketing strategies, we’re able to provide our clients with the resources they need to succeed in the global marketplace.

Whether you are looking to expand your business into the global market or just starting out, our consultants can help you navigate the international trade landscape. From market research and compliance to product sourcing and product selection, we have the expertise to ensure your success.

We also provide specialized services such as customs clearance, logistics, and consulting on international trade regulations. Our team is always up-to-date with the latest trends in Ethiopia and global trade, so you can trust us for the best advice on how to maximize profits and minimize risks.

At Nohaya, our goal is to provide our clients with the best possible advice and guidance for their import-export business. We understand that each business has unique needs, and we strive to tailor our services to meet those needs.

​ We understand the complexities of international trade. Our team of experienced professionals have years of experience in the import-export industry, marketing and are ready to provide you with the expert advice and guidance you need to succeed.
